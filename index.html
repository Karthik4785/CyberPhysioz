Spinal Manipulation

It is used to treat Back pain, Neck pain and other Musculoskeletal conditions via the application of force to the spinal dysfunctioning area.

Cupping

This involves placing cups on the skin to create suction.This suction can facilitate healing with blood flow

Kinesio Tapping

This type of treatment helps improve muscle contractions,nerve receptors,posture and reduces swelling,pain,scarred tissue.

Modalities

This includes a range of treatment using electricity to Reduce pain, Improve circulation, Repair tissues, Strengthen muscles and helps to improve physical functions.

Dry Needling

This is a modern treatment designed to ease muscular pain.Fine,short stainless steel needles are inserted into your trigger points of muscle to ease pain.
